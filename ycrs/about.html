    • About Us

    "Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self." - The Bhagavad Gita

    Welcome to our yoga class registration page! We’re excited that you’re considering joining us for a journey of self-discovery and transformation through the practice of yoga.

    Our classes are led by experienced instructors who are dedicated to guiding you through a safe and transformative yoga practice. Whether you’re new to yoga or have been practicing for years, our classes are designed to meet you where you’re at and challenge you to grow in your practice.

    We offer a variety of yoga classes to fit your needs and schedule, including vinyasa, hatha, yin, restorative, and more. Each class is tailored to the needs of the students in attendance, and our instructors offer modifications and adjustments to ensure that you are practicing safely and effectively.

    In addition to our regular classes, we also offer workshops, events, and retreats that dive deeper into specific aspects of yoga, meditation, and wellness. These are great opportunities to deepen your practice and connect with a community of like-minded individuals.

    To register for our classes, simply browse our schedule and select the class that works best for you. You can register online or in person at our studio.

    Thank you for considering our yoga classes as a part of your wellness journey. We look forward to seeing you on the mat!
